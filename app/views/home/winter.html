Winter fishing

In Russia, ice fishing is usually referred to as ice fishing, while in countries with a milder climate, it can also take place on open water. Passionate about this kind recreation is popularly called "penguins": not everyone understands what a pleasure can be obtained by sitting for hours in the cold near an open hole.
